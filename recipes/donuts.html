Donuts

Description

If you have made it this far, that means you have crafted a magnificent pizza, and a gigantic spaghetti. Now it is time for dessert.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Frosting of your choosing
  • Sprinkles

Steps

  1. Roll your dough into a large blob.
  2. Rip off chunks of the dough that are the proper size for your donuts.
  3. Roll the donuts into a ball that is relatively flat.
  4. Poke a large donut sized hole into each donut and place them in the oven for 30 minutes.
  5. Once they are finished baking, place the frosting on top and cover with sprinkles.
  6. Enjoy.
