Pizza

Description

This will be the greatest pizza you have ever created. It will not be easy. You will need extreme focus and skill to accomplish this task.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Pepperoni
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Other toppings of your choosing

Steps

  1. Place your pizza dough on a large pan.
  2. Cover the dough with enough sauce.
  3. Add a lot of cheese on top of the sauce.
  4. Place the pepperoni/other toppings on top.
  5. Place in oven for 30 minutes.
