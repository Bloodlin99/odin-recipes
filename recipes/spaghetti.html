Spaghetti

Description

Behold. The largest spaghetti you have ever laid eyes upon. If you wish to create such a massive spaghetti with your own hands, continue reading.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Meatballs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water and begin boiling it on the stove.
  2. Once the water is boiling place the noodles inside until they have cooked.
  3. While the noodles are cooking, begin to heat up the sauce.
  4. Cook the meatballs while you are waiting.
  5. Once everything is finished, combine all of it and place the parmesan cheese on top.
